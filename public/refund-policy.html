Refund Policy

Last Updated: June 10, 2026

General Policy

Contributions made through the Class Fund Manager are generally collected for class activities, programs, and expenses. Because these funds are pooled for shared class benefits, refunds are handled under specific conditions outlined below.

Refund Eligibility

Refunds may be considered in the following scenarios:

  • A duplicate payment was accidentally processed.
  • A payment was made in error (e.g., incorrect amount entered).
  • The specific program or event for which the contribution was collected has been officially cancelled.
  • The Class Representative or Administrator approves a special refund request.

Non-Refundable Cases

Refunds may not be available when:

  • The collected funds have already been utilized for their intended purpose.
  • The event or activity has already occurred.
  • Program expenses have already been incurred and cannot be recovered by the class fund.

Refund Request Process

If you believe you are eligible for a refund, students must contact the Class Representatives or Administrators with the following details:

  • Full Name
  • Roll Number
  • Razorpay Payment ID
  • Clear reason for the refund request

Refund Processing Time

Once a refund is approved by the administrators, it will be processed directly through Razorpay.

Expected processing time: 5–10 business days depending on banking channels.

Contact Information

To request a refund or if you have questions about a payment, please visit our Contact page.
